GAMEPLAY

THE PATH INTO THE ABYSS is a next-gen Horror Survival Shooter based on the dark horror universe created by filmmaker MATÍAS RISPAU, The Path Into The Abyss is set to deliver a unique action experience of survival in the mysterious abyss that surrounds the City Of Buenos Aires.

Experience a first person shooter gameplay, featuring elements of an RPG, sandbox and horror.

Play a major part in a story to unveil the secrets of a local dark apocalyptic fiction. Bear your right to reclaim the rule over the abyss & lead your brothers into the darkness.

Zero tolerance for SKILL ISSUES. Be advice javaScripters and others alike, life on the abyss could be a little harsh.

    The Abyss

    The Abyss has never been so alive as it is now. The abyss is a dark place located in a gloomy environment in Greater Buenos Aires.

    Embark on a bloody journey through the sinister open world of the abyss.

    THE PATH INTO THE ABYSS takes full advantage of the potential of Unreal Engine, delivering cutting-edge graphics and unparalleled immersion into the game world.

    Its advanced artificial intelligence system forces the survivor to plan ahead when approaching enemies. The world simulation system creates a comprehensive environment in which the player's actions alter the abyss.

    Monsters and humans won't be the only dangers you’ll face.

    You will witness the challenges that survivors experienced while consuming scarce supplies, such as food, drink, medication, or alcohol.

    Survival mechanics enhance the gameplay that tells this horror story. Will you be able to unravel the hidden secrets of this universe?
